# Validation Plan

## 30-day decision

Forge Loop must prove its core loop quickly.

The prototype is not judged by how much content it has. It is judged by whether the loop is worth replaying.

## Internal test

After first playable loop, play 5 full runs.

Record:

- Did I understand the outcome?
- Did I make meaningful decisions?
- Did I want another run?
- What felt boring?
- What felt satisfying?
- What was confusing?

## External test

Test with 5 people.

Ask:

1. What did you think the goal was?
2. What confused you?
3. Would you play another run?
4. What was the best moment?
5. What was the most boring moment?

Do not ask:
- Do you like it?
- Would you buy it?
- Should I keep going?

## Continue criteria

Continue if:

- You personally replay it voluntarily.
- 3 of 5 testers want another run.
- Confusion is mostly UI/rules explanation.
- The grid creates interesting optimisation decisions.

## Kill criteria

Kill or pivot if:

- Players understand it but feel nothing.
- The fun only appears after adding more features.
- You need lore/art/monetisation to make it seem interesting.
- You dread playing it yourself.
